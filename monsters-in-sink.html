Monsters in the Sink

Main | About Her Furriness | What's In A Name?

One of Malla's favorite pastimes is investigating the mysterious creatures that dwell in the depths of the kitchen sink. These elusive monsters only appear when the water is running, creating whirlpools and splashing about in what can only be described as their natural habitat.

With keen hunter's eyes, Malla will crouch at the edge of the sink, tail twitching with anticipation, waiting for the perfect moment to strike at these aquatic beasts. Her paw will delicately dip into the swirling waters, sending ripples across the surface as she attempts to catch her slippery prey.

Often, she'll succeed in catching a few droplets on her paw, which she'll then lick off with great satisfaction, as if she's just captured the most delicious of fish. The monsters, however, remain ever elusive, disappearing the moment the water stops flowing, only to return another day for a new adventure.
